Our Story

A Celebration of Light, Unity & Creativity

The Starlight Lantern & Arts Festival began as a small neighborhood gathering and has blossomed into one of Brunswick's most anticipated annual celebrations.

Our festival operates on a sustainable foundation. All light installations utilize energy-efficient LED modules powered by 100% renewable solar generators, and we enforce a strict single-use plastic ban across all vendor stalls.
