Location

Riverside Park, Brunswick

This year, the festival expands along the banks of the scenic Merri Creek in Riverside Park, Brunswick. Over two kilometers of walking tracks are turned into glowing corridors of art installations and interactive zones.

  • Address: 120 Riverside Drive, Brunswick VIC 3056
  • Entry: Free (registration recommended for attendance tracking)
  • Dates: October 16 – 18, 2026
  • Opening Hours: 5:00 PM – 11:00 PM Daily

Please note: The festival area is fully accessible to wheelchair users, strollers, and service animals. High-speed guest Wi-Fi points are active throughout the festival layout.

Transport Guide

How to Get Here

We strongly encourage visitors to walk, cycle, or use Melbourne's public transport system, as parking spaces are highly limited.

🚇

By Train

Brunswick Railway Station (Upfield Line) is the closest station. It is a scenic 7-minute walk to the park's East Entry. Lantern trail wayfinding pillars will guide you from the exit.

🚌

By Bus

Bus Route 508 (Gisborne - Alphington) runs directly along Victoria Street and stops outside the main entry gate. Extra night shuttle services are scheduled for Friday and Saturday nights.

🚋

By Tram

Route 96 (East Brunswick) stops at the terminal loop on Nicholson Street. From there, follow the illuminated path westward down Victoria Street for approximately 10 minutes.

🚲

Bicycle & Parking

Bicycle racks are installed at both Main and East entries. Limited street parking is available, alongside a designated drop-off/rideshare zone at the North gate.
